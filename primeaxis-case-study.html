THE STORY

Project Overview

Executive Summary

PrimeAxis delivered a comprehensive property management SaaS platform for Elite Realty Group, transforming fragmented operations into a unified, intelligent ecosystem.

Project Objectives

  • → Streamline property listings and tenant management
  • → Automate lead nurturing and conversion
  • → Provide real-time analytics dashboard

Client Goals

Modernize legacy systems, reduce manual administrative work by over 50%, and create a scalable platform for future growth.

📋 Business Requirements

Multi-property portfolio management, financial tracking, maintenance scheduling, and tenant portal.

⚙️ Functional Requirements

Role-based access, automated notifications, advanced search, document management, and seamless integrations.

Expected Outcome

A powerful, intuitive platform that empowers property managers to focus on growth rather than operations.

Business Challenges

The obstacles that shaped our approach

Fragmented Systems

Multiple disconnected tools leading to data silos and inefficiencies.

Slow Lead Response

Manual processes resulted in lost opportunities and poor customer experience.

“We were drowning in spreadsheets and missed leads daily. We needed one system to rule them all.”
- CEO, Elite Realty Group
